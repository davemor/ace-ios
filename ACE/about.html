About ARC

ARC-Edinburgh was a winning entry in the 2014 Edinburgh Apps competition. Since then, Anne-Marie Mann, Ella Robbins and app developer, David Morrison have been working with Edinburgh City Council and Edinburgh Drug & Alcohol Partnership in consultation with the recovery community in Edinburgh to create an app to support those in recovery in Edinburgh.
